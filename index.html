Backup and privacy

Your diary stays in this browser unless you export it. PhactoryFit has no account system, analytics, advertising SDK, or private API key.

What leaves this device?

Camera frames and barcode photos are processed locally and are not uploaded. When you search packaged foods or scan an unknown barcode, the search text or barcode number is sent to Open Food Facts to retrieve nutrition data. Voice search is provided by Safari and may use Apple's online speech-processing service.

For strongest privacy on GitHub Pages, deploy PhactoryFit on a dedicated custom domain so its browser storage is isolated from other projects on the same github.io origin. See PRIVACY.md and SECURITY.md.
